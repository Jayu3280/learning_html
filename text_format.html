History - Sivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630-1680) was a Maratha warrior king and founder of the Maratha Empire in India . He is revered for his military prowess, innovative strategies, and his contribution to the decline of Mughal rule. Shivaji was born in Shivneri Fort Shivner Fort, Maharashtra, to Shahaji Bhosale and Jijabai. He was raised under the guidance of his mother and Brahmin Dadaji Konda-dev, who instilled in him a strong sense of justice and leadership.
